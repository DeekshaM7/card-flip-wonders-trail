7 Wonders of the World

The Great Wall of China, built to protect China from invasions.
Petra, Jordan: An ancient city known for its rock-cut architecture.
Christ the Redeemer, Brazil: A famous statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro.
Machu Picchu, Peru: An Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains.
Chichen Itza, Mexico: A large pre-Columbian archaeological site.
Colosseum, Italy: An ancient amphitheater in Rome, known for gladiator battles.
Taj Mahal, India: A white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan.
